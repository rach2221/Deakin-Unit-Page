SIT223-SIT753 - Professional Practice in IT

Content

As students, this unit provides an overview of industry tools, professional practices, and teamwork skills required in IT environments. It helps students understand how different technologies connect together while developing ethical awareness, collaboration skills, and problem-solving abilities. This allows students to gain a clear understanding of expectations and prepares them for real-world IT work.

Hurdle Requirement

To be eligible to pass this unit, students must complete all required portfolio tasks and meet the specified assessment criteria. These requirements ensure that students demonstrate practical understanding and engagement throughout the unit.
